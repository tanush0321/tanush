# Project Overview
This Digital Literacy Project consists of five tasks designed to enhance understanding of digital tools, online safety, communication, and professional presence. The project focuses on developing essential skills required to effectively and responsibly use digital technologies in academic and professional environments.

---

# Tasks Overview

### Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
Created a one-page infographic using Canva to explain digital literacy, useful tools, safe internet practices, and email etiquette. The aim was to present information in a clear and visually engaging manner.

---

### Task 2: Build Your Student Digital Portfolio
Developed a digital portfolio to showcase academic achievements, skills, and projects. This task emphasized creating a professional online identity and organizing personal work effectively.

---

### Task 3: Explore Coding & Collaboration Platforms
Explored platforms like GitHub and other coding tools to understand version control, collaboration, and project management. Learned how developers work together on projects efficiently.

---

### Task 4: Draft a Professional Email & Etiquette Guide
Created a guide explaining how to write professional emails, including structure, tone, subject lines, and proper communication etiquette.

---

### Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness Case Study & Prevention Guide
Studied cybercrime cases and prepared a guide on common cyber threats and preventive measures. This task highlighted the importance of cybersecurity and safe online behavior.

## Summary of Learning
Through these tasks, I gained a strong understanding of digital literacy and its importance in today’s world.
