Problem Statement

Schools and educational institutions face significant challenges in managing student fee records manually using paper registers or spreadsheets. This manual process is time-consuming, error-prone, and inefficient. Administrative staff waste valuable time searching through registers to find student fee details and calculating pending amounts. 

Scope of the Project
The Fee Management System provides a complete digital solution for tracking student fees in educational institutions. The scope includes:

 Student registration with unique roll numbers, names, and grade information
 Recording total fee amounts for each student
 Tracking partial and full fee payments
 Automatic calculation of pending fee amounts
 Searching and viewing individual student fee details

Out of Scope
 Multiple user accounts or authentication system
 Fee payment history with dates and timestamps
 Different fee categories (tuition, transport, hostel)
 Generating printed receipts or reports
 Email or SMS notifications for due payments

Target Users
Primary Users
1. School Administrators - Manage overall fee collection and generate reports
2. Accountants/Fee Clerks - Handle daily fee transactions and record payments
3. Office Staff - Add new students and update student information
4. Principal/Management - Review fee collection status and pending amounts

 Secondary Users
1. Small Coaching Centers - Track tuition fees for batch students
2. Tutorial Classes - Manage monthly fee payments
3. Private Schools - Small institutions with 50-500 students

 User Characteristics
 Basic computer literacy required
 No programming knowledge needed
 Comfortable with command-line interface

 User Requirements
 Simple and easy-to-understand interface
 Fast data entry and retrieval

High-Level Features

 1. Student Registration
 Add new students with unique roll numbers
 Store student name, grade, and fee details

 2. Fee Payment Processing
 Record partial payments
 Record full payments

3. Student Information Management
 Search student by roll number
 View complete student details
 Update student name

4. Fee Tracking and Reporting
 Display individual student fee status
Show list of all students with pending fees
 Identify students who have fully paid
